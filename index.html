Self-Employment Tax Note: If your net profit from comic sales exceeds $400/year (US), the IRS generally considers this a business and requires Schedule C filing. For amounts over ~$1,000/year, you may need to make quarterly estimated tax payments (April 15, June 15, Sept 15, Jan 15). This is ~25–30% of net profit for most filers.

Quarterly Breakdown — Track income and expenses per quarter to calculate estimated tax payments. The US self-employment tax rate is ~15.3% on net profit, plus income tax.
Always consult a CPA. The information below is general educational guidance only — not personalized advice.
Do I need to pay taxes on comic sales?

Hobby vs. Business: The IRS distinguishes between a hobby and a business. If you sell with the intent to make a profit and do so regularly, it's likely a business (Schedule C). Hobbies have fewer deductions.

Key thresholds (US, 2024):
• eBay/PayPal will send a 1099-K if you receive over $5,000 in payments (2024 threshold)
• Net self-employment income over $400 requires Schedule SE
• Quarterly payments suggested when you'll owe over $1,000 in a year

What you can deduct: Cost of comics purchased for resale, shipping/packing materials, eBay/platform fees, bags/boards, storage, mileage to comic shops, portion of phone/internet used for the business, and any professional services.

Record keeping: Keep receipts for at least 3 years (7 if you under-report income). Use this app to log every purchase and expense.

Data Sources — Full transparency on where every piece of information comes from.

① FREE
Open Library / ISBN
openlibrary.org — No key needed. Best for TPBs, hardcovers, and collected editions. ISBN barcodes start with 978 or 979. Covers all publishers.
② FREE
UPC Item DB
upcitemdb.com — No key needed. Consumer product database covering Image Comics, Dark Horse, IDW, Valiant, BOOM!, indie publishers as well as Marvel and DC. Best general-purpose fallback.
③ KEY
Comic Vine
comicvine.gamespot.com — 1M+ issues, all publishers. Now uses direct UPC field lookup (more accurate than before). Requires free API key in Settings. Returns cover art and key issue notes.
④ FREE
Wikidata / Grand Comics Database
query.wikidata.org — No key needed. GCD data mirrored in Wikidata via UPC barcode property. Good for identified key issues across all publishers.
⑤ AI
AI Specialist Fallback
Claude AI — primed with UPC prefix knowledge for Image (70985), Dark Horse (76156), IDW (82771), Valiant (85767), BOOM! (84428), Marvel (75960), DC (76194). Best effort — not guaranteed.
